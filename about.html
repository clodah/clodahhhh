ABOUT THE LMC 2024

The LMC Event at TCGC School is a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and academic excellence. It brings together students to showcase their skills through thrilling performance competitions and challenging academic contests. This event not only fosters healthy competition but also highlights the hard work and dedication of every participant. LMC is a testament to TCGC School's commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel both in academics and the arts.
